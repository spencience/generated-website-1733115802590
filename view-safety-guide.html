Bear Safety Guide

Essential Safety Tips

Before Your Trip

  • Research the area and local bear species
  • Travel in groups whenever possible
  • Carry bear spray and know how to use it
  • Make noise while hiking to avoid surprise encounters

During an Encounter

  • Stay calm and slowly back away
  • Do not run - bears can outrun humans
  • Make yourself appear large
  • Speak calmly but firmly to the bear

Camp Safety

  • Store food in bear-proof containers
  • Keep cooking areas away from sleeping areas
  • Never keep food in your tent
  • Dispose of garbage properly

Emergency Contacts

  • Park Rangers: 1-800-BEAR-HELP
  • Wildlife Emergency: 911
